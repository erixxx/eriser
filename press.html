Edge Risers: snowboarding accessory that can greatly improve the way you ride

Loveland, CO (February 8, 2013) - An IndieGoGo campaign, which has just recently started, is promoting a new snowboarding accessory called the Edge Risers, which improves the way you ride a snowboard. The campaign is run by father and son, professionals in their industries. They have a month to come up with the funding for what they say to be "an exciting announcement for snowboarders everywhere".

The Edge Risers are a special binding riser that lifts your boot and binding up further off the board, thus giving you a number of different advantages. According to their sleek new website, edge-risers.com, the Edge Risers help improve your riding ability in a number of ways, such as drastically helping those who may have larger feet from getting toe and heel drag; improving your carving responsiveness; and giving your board a better flex during turns. When the IndieGoGo campaign is over, Edge Risers will be sent out to those whom contributed enough to purchase their own pair of edge risers.

Val Strals is the proud owner of the plastic injection mold machining company Horizon Precision Machining Inc. Val has been machining parts and making molds for over 35 years. He works with a range of clients, ranging from large aerospace engineering companies to local inventors needing help getting started with the machining and molding process. Eriks Strals is a creative software engineer and inventor who has been working in the tech and games industry for more then 6 years.

Together, Val and Eriks have been snowboarding for over 20 years and working on the Edge Risers for the last 5 years, trying to get them out to the world and in the hands of the snowboarders who need them. To find out more information about their IndieGoGo campaign, go to: IndieGoGO or check out edge-risers.com.
